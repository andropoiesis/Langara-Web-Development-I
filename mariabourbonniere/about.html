About Maria Bourbonniere

Maria Bourbonniere is a Canadian author and illustrator of children's books.

Maria Bourbonniere started writing after the birth of her daughter. Her daughter loved being read to and would sit and listen intently as these stories were read aloud to her. She would also read these stories to the students in her daughter's classes in grade school. The storybook characters would come alive in the imagination of the little girl.

Maria's books use animal characters that children can easily identify with. The stories provide entertaining and positive messages.
