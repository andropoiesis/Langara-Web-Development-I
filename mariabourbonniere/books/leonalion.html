Leona the Lioness Meets Mighty Egor the Elephant

Leona the Lioness Meets Mighty Egor the Elephant is Maria's second book and was published in 2011. Its message is not to judge others by their appearances, but by their actions. We can live happily among others, if we make an effort to understand each other.

Where to find Leona the Lioness Meets Mighty Egor the Elephant

Leona the Lioness Meets Mighty Egor the Elephant is available in hardcover in most Canadian book stores and can be ordered online from international book sellers and through the following sellers:
